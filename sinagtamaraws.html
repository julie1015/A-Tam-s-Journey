About Sinag Tamaraws

Sinag Tamaraws is a collective celebration of teamwork, perseverance, and the incredible strength of the Tamaraw community. This event brings together individuals from various walks of life to showcase their talents, teamwork, and unity.

Through this event, we aim to inspire others to embrace their culture, push boundaries, and support one another in all endeavors. It is an event where the Tamaraw spirit shines bright.
